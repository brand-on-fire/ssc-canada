Camp Moses

Camp Moses brandonfire 2018-04-05T17:10:43+00:00

Camp Moses specializes in the design, operation and management of Canadian winter and summer camps. It specializes in BC tourism projects such as Art & Culture, Business, Technology & Engineering, Nature & Environment, such as university tours, language excursions, and outdoor development. Leadership tours, etc., particularly host the successful experience of the winter/summer camps of well-known Chinese institutions.

CampMoses students have the wonderful opportunity of being able to go outside and explore the Canadian outdoors through a variety of professionally organized and free-time activities in our winter and summer camps. We specialize in Canadian British Columbia tourism projects such as Art & Culture, Business, Technology & Engineering, Nature & Environment, as well as university tours, language excursions, and outdoor development.

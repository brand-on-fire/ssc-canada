ESL & IELTS

ESL & IELTS brandonfire 2018-04-05T17:15:14+00:00

In Toronto and Vancouver, private language schools are one of the highest quality education opportunities.  Every year, students from all over the world go to private language schools for short-term study or long-term study opportunities for English language training. Private language schools generally have the following benefits:

  1. Convenient transportation and generally concentrated in the Downtown Vancouver area
  2. In small classes, there are generally no more than 15 people, with an average of 10 students.
  3. Mixed nationality: Germany, Swiss, Brazilian, Japanese, South Korean, etc.
  4. The proportion of Chinese students is relatively small, and the language teaching environment is good.
  5. Many ESL schools are divided into 10 levels so students of various levels can be arranged to learn in groups of their same level.
  6. Courses range from basic ESL to business English, subject preparation for English, IELTS/TOEFL, and individual listening, speaking, reading and writing skills.
  • General English
  • Academic English/University Preparatory English
  • Business English
  • English for Specific Purpose (Medical/Legal/Technical etc.)
  • Group Programs (English)
  • Summer or Winter Activity or Camp Programs (English)
  • Youth / Junior programs (English)
  • Cambridge Test Preparation
  • TOEFL Preparation
  • TOEIC Preparation
  • Other Programs (English)
  • IELTS Preparation
  1. The teaching is rigorous. Most of the professors have business experience, completed undergraduate education, and teaching qualification certificates.
  2. Extracurricular activities are rich and varied. The formal language schools not only focus on English, but also provide a variety of extracurricular and holiday activities to meet the ability of students from various countries to learn and use English.
  3. The study period is flexible. One week or one year, full-time or half-day, depending on individual needs.
  4. Regular language schools are members of language Canada, PCTIA, and are certified by EQA.

Of course, choosing a language school is an important decision. How do you choose a language school that is effective, reliable, and quality?

SSC-Canada’s consultants provide free advice for parents, students, and advisors.

Student Experiences

Student #1

I made many friends here. Some of them are Chinese and some of them are from other countries like Japan, Korea, Mexico and Brazil. I have learned about their different countries and cultures.
I have to say something about my amazing Chinese counselor. Her Name is Ellen. She is kind and caring, and she helped me to figure out many new cultural things. She also held a Chinese New Year lunch, and that was so cool!!!!!!!!!

Student #2

I want to thank my counselor Ellen. She actually helped me a lot to learn about this new culture and country. I always went to her office to speak with her, and she’s very friendly and taught me many things that I didn’t know before.

Student #3

My counselor Ellen helped me a lot with my classes, gave me advice about how to choose the right classes, and helped me understand many other things about this new place. In my free time, I always went to the office to speak with her, and because of her I got more and more used to the new study environment. I loved getting help from SSC-Canada.

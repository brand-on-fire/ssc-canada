SSC-CANADA

SSC-CANADA brandonfire 2018-04-05T17:12:53+00:00

SSC-Canada focuses on overseas academic training courses, including etiquette for North American business and culture, outdoor survival, sports, and English trainings.

SSC-Canada also provides international students with post-placement placement service in Vancouver, VIP study, life supervision, and homestay placement (for international students studying abroad).

SSC-CANADA has a passionate team of change-makers who provide professional development workshops, experiential programming and consultation services to increase our students’ success, interest and perspective. Our teachers and facilitators provide unique knowledge, experience, ideals and values of their own background to help participants create their own productive, practical, and personal “toolbox” to succeed in both work and life.  We provide the tools that will inspire your student to meet their potential.

SSC-CANADA offers extensive training in North American etiquette, including dining, formal and informal events, professional behavior, and a wide range of other situations.  Our students go through rigorous hands-on situational education that involves discussion and theory that can be applied to real-world situations.

We host special events that are specifically focused around educating students on the proper etiquette for all situations in North American and Western culture.  We discuss common mistakes and how to avoid them, how to correct etiquette mistakes, and how to use observation skills in order to continue self-guided learning in how to use proper etiquette.

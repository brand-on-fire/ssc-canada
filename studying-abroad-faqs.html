What is the guardian of an international student?

Study abroad schedule:

  1. The student chooses the appropriate area and school, fills in the application form and pays an application fee of CAD 500.
  2. The school issues a “conditional admission notice” and the center is responsible for mailing to parents or studying abroad.
  3. After the student fully pays all the money, the school issues the “Admission Notice”.
  4. Center notary “legal guardian documents.”
  5. The center mailed the “Admission Notice” and “Legal Guardian Document.”
  6. Students apply for a visa.
  7. After the visa is successful, the center will pay the guardianship fee.
  8. After the student purchases the ticket, the notice center, the center arranges to pick up the plane and provides the information of the pick-up person to the parent.
  9. After the student arrives, the center will report the situation of the student to the parents within 24 hours.
  10. Before the school starts, the center will take students to the school registration to understand the situation of the school and help students choose courses.
  11. Within one week after the school starts, the center will send parents the students a class schedule for the current semester.
  12. From the sixth week to the eighth week of school, the center will first communicate with the teacher at the school and send a communication report to parents.
  13. After the transcript of the mid-term exam is announced, the center will mail the transcripts to parents.
  14. Within three months after the school starts, the center will visit the students for the second time to understand the learning situation and send a communication report to parents.
  15. Within three weeks after the final exam, the center sends the first semester final exam report card to parents.
  16. At the beginning of the second semester, the center will send the curriculum to parents.
  17. From the sixth week to the eighth week after the school starts, the center will visit the students for the third time and send a communication report to parents.
  18. Within three weeks after the mid-term exam, the center sent the test scores to parents.
  19. Between March and April, the center will help students who continue their studies to renew their VISA.
